Home page

I'm gonna have a picture of me so people know what I look like. And I'm planning on having a summary about what I do on the home page. On the home page I am going to have three tabs. They will be called home, my work, and contact me. Since I have such diverse skills in technology. I will put the list of skills I have and what I can provide to the company or person or whoever is looking at my page. I will also have a quick summary that would say something like,

Personable and knowledgeable IT support specialist with over 12 years of experience assisting users with various hardware, software, and network connectivity related issues. Efficient in explaining technical IT concepts and procedures to non-technical users. Love learning new skills and information every day. Not afraid to do the research needed to increase my knowledge and abilities

People always tell me that my ability to learn quickly, work hard and being personable, and be liked by everyone makes me a great employee. Indeed, I've been successful at nearly everything I've done. I am a hardworking and very determined individual. I make things happen. I am a go-getter; I am motivated by success. I loved pushing myself and learning new things! When someone says I can't do something, I will do everything I am able to, I show them that I can. I am always on time. I presume with your company, customer service is a top priority and that is what helps drive your company. In every one of my jobs, I have always tried to make people comfortable around me and help them with anything that I can. I believe having exceptional customer service skills is great quality to have and is one that companies, along with yours, really looks for. I know I would be an asset to your company. Thank you for your time and consideration.
